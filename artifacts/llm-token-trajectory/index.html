Methodology & provenance

Google · Reported

Sundar Pichai disclosed monthly token volume at three consecutive I/O keynotes: 9.7T (May 2024), 480T (May 2025), 3,200T (May 2026). Spans all surfaces: Gemini app, Search AI Overviews, API, and internal developer use.

Source: blog.google · I/O 2026 keynote

OpenAI · Reported (API only)

Numbers are tokens/minute on the API, multiplied to monthly. 2023: 300M/min ≈ 13T/mo. Oct 2025: 6B/min ≈ 260T/mo. April 2026: 15B/min ≈ 650T/mo. Excludes ChatGPT consumer traffic, which OpenAI doesn't break out separately.

Source: openai.com · $122B funding announcement · DevDay 2025

Anthropic · Derived

Anthropic does not publish token volume. Estimated from disclosed revenue divided by blended token price. Revenue: ~$9B ARR end of 2025, $14B Feb 2026, $30B+ April 2026. Using ~$8/M blended (accounting for cached input, batch discounts, and the input/output mix), monthly tokens are roughly the monthly revenue times 125,000.

Wide uncertainty band: actual figure could be ±50%. Independent third party estimates put Claude at 40% of enterprise LLM spend in Dec 2025, ahead of OpenAI's 27%.

Sources: Sacra revenue tracker · Menlo Ventures Dec 2025 report

Comparability caveat

These three numbers measure slightly different things. Google's is total across all surfaces; OpenAI's is API only (ChatGPT consumer is on top); Anthropic's is derived. Direct comparison overstates Google relative to OpenAI. The trajectory shape is more meaningful than absolute differences.

What counts as a "token"?

A token is roughly 4 characters or 0.75 of an English word. One million tokens is about 750,000 words. A typical chat message is 50–200 tokens. A complex agentic coding task can burn 1–3 million tokens with retries.

Reasoning models inflate token counts. A single "Hi" to a reasoning model can trigger dozens of internal steps before responding, each counted. Gemini 2.5 Flash uses roughly 17 times more tokens per request than its predecessor.

This means recent token growth reflects both more usage and more tokens consumed per request. The curve overstates pure user growth.
